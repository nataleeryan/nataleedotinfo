AUG 2019. The above drawing was inspired by the song "HOTTIE" by Brockhampton. I wanted to see if I could tell the story of how the idea came to be through some sort of spread, making the process into art on its own.




JULY 2019. Mid-summer, my ideas felt like they just floated on away from me. I saw a really cool beer can on a trip to Korea this summer with a beautiful color palette, inspiring this piece during a creative bust.




MAY 2019. This 3-part series was inspired by one of my favorite rising artists, Kevin Abstract. He released a collection with these words and I wanted to see what those words would look like if I threw some collage art to it.




JUNE 2019. For my Information Design class, we had a few assignments I enjoyed more than others. One being redesigning a document (boarding pass, left) and visualizing some sort of email data (pictured right).




DEC 2018. This piece was to practice continuity in a series through texture and color. This became one of my favorite pieces, putting it into art shows and my friends putting it on their walls. The idea was to make ordinary things look extra-ordinary.
